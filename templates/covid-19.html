Covid-19

Overview

Covid-19 is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It primarily spreads through respiratory droplets. Symptoms may range from mild to severe. Vaccination is a critical tool in controlling the virus. New variants may emerge, requiring updated vaccines. Public health measures remain essential for prevention.

  • Deaths: 6.5 million+
